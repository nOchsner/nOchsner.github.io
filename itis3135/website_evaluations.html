Website Evaluations


Youtube


This website is used for streaming videos.


  • Contrast, Depending on if you're on light or dark mode it either has a black background and white text or white background and black text. Pretty good contrast.
  • Repetition, On my device there were three videos per row repeated as you scroll down. Split into repeated sections of different types of videos
  • Alignment, For me the videos were three in every row and well spaced out to define one from another. Clear and easy to understand layout.
  • Proximity, Groups related videos get put together in sections and options and menu items are grouped together at different parts of the page

Mini Crossword


Used to play the Daily Mini Crossword.


  • Contrast, White background with black text, simple but it definitely has contrast.
  • Repetition, Good repition of listing clues and other games and info at the bottom.
  • Alignment, This is where I drew issue with this site. When completing the mini on my pc because if you were to fit te crossword to screen then there's a huge blank space to the right. It is annoying to look to the right for the next clue only to need to look up a bit more because you looked at the wrong spot.
  • Proximity, Also does well with proximity by keeping related items like clues or other available games close together in sections.
